GCP4 (2013.02)

GCP4 means "Global Challenge Program 4th", and the destination was Bangalore, India. In this program I learned about global bussiness from speakers, visited companies in Bangalore, did internship, and proceed a project. The content of the project was marketing a Japanese Fashion magazine in India, and looking for some business partner in India. Our team researched the market (especially interviewed local people), and visited lots of publishing companies. Finally, we listed up some candidates and submitted reports to the publisher who publishes the fashion magazine.
